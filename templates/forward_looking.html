3-Month Forward Looking Analysis

Area-level booking and revenue outlook across all 25 operational areas for March through June. The Mar 26 column shows actual current-month performance — how many appointments are booked relative to what we would expect at this point in the month, and the current practice RAG split. The Apr, May and Jun columns show forecast performance, modelled from each practice's historical booking curve, NHS/Private mix, and expected price uplifts. Progress bars show the booking rate and revenue delivery against AOP — green ≥ 90% / 97%, amber is in range, red is below target. Use the Region and Month filters to narrow the view.
